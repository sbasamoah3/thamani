Our Investment Approach

A single style.
Relentlessly applied.

At Thamani, our entire organisation is united around one objective — long-term investment in Africa's secular growth businesses with durable competitive advantages, run by exceptional management teams.

01

Idea Generation

Our portfolio managers and research analysts are our single most important source of idea generation. With roots in deep pan-African industry research, we have cultivated expertise across every sector and market we cover.

Learn More
  • Proprietary access via direct management contact across 30+ African markets
  • On-the-ground research presence in Accra, Lagos, Nairobi, and Johannesburg
  • Bottom-up screening of 2,000+ listed equities across African exchanges
  • Cross-pollination between sector specialists and regional analysts
  • Long-term thematic research on Africa's structural growth drivers
02

Research

The goal of our research process is to identify and monitor investments that will deliver above-average, long-term, risk-adjusted results. We invest in understanding a business over years, not quarters.

Learn More
  • Proprietary financial modelling with 5–10 year horizon
  • Competitive moat assessment tailored to African market dynamics
  • Management quality, governance, and capital allocation evaluation
  • Channel checks, industry surveys, and primary on-the-ground research
  • Continuous monitoring of thesis integrity post-investment
03

Portfolio Construction

Every Thamani portfolio is built bottom-up, stock by stock, based on our conviction about each business's long-term growth prospects. Position sizing reflects conviction, not index weights.

Learn More
  • High-conviction, concentrated portfolios of 20–35 names
  • Benchmark-agnostic allocation driven by upside/risk ratio
  • Active position sizing based on variant perception and valuation
  • Low turnover philosophy — typically under 20% annually
  • Currency and country risk managed at portfolio construction
04

Risk Management

We define risk as the probability of permanent capital loss — not short-term tracking error. Our discipline is enforced at every stage of the investment process with deep awareness of Africa-specific risk factors.

Learn More
  • Downside scenario analysis including FX, political, and liquidity shocks
  • Country risk concentration limits across portfolio
  • Governance and ESG review with African regulatory context
  • Valuation discipline at entry — margin of safety always required
  • Ongoing monitoring of balance sheet risk and management integrity

A Message from Our Founder

Built on conviction.
Proven over decades.

For over two decades, Thamani Investment has operated on a single, unchanging belief: that exceptional African businesses, held with patience and discipline, create extraordinary wealth over time. Our culture is built around this conviction — from how we hire, to how we research, to how we partner with the companies we own.
